ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Academic Project Management System is designed to manage, store and obtain project information that are used in web-based applications. Our clients are students who will follow and followed courses in ATI - Kandy and who is involved with the courses in ATI - Kandy. Problem is that, projects/Assignments of the students may have lost anytime for the reason that their projects/Assignments are stored only their storage devices and cannot get it instantly for the future needs. Thus upcoming students cannot get an idea from there seniors projects. This project is done as a website for students. Registered students can store, manage and obtain their projects here. So it will be useful to the upcoming students for their future project ideas.


Why This Website was Created ?

"Till now there is no proper project management system in our ATI. In that case new students cannot able to get an idea from their seniors projects. So we plan to make a project management system as a website. With the help of that website, upcoming students can get some benefits for their projects, get ideas for projects and can get solution for their problems."
